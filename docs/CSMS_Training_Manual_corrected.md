## 1. Introduction to CSMS

### 1.1 What is CSMS?

The **Civil Service Management System (CSMS)** is a web-based platform designed to automate and streamline Human Resource management processes for the Civil Service Commission of Zanzibar. The system manages HR operations for over 50,000 employees across all government institutions.

**Key Benefits:**

- ✅ **Faster Processing:** 70% reduction in request processing time
- ✅ **Paperless Operations:** Complete digital workflow
- ✅ **Transparency:** Real-time status tracking
- ✅ **Accuracy:** Reduced errors through validation
- ✅ **Security:** Role-based access and complete audit trail
- ✅ **Accessibility:** 24/7 web access from anywhere

### 1.2 System Overview

**CSMS manages:**

- Employee profiles and documents
- Employment confirmation requests
- Promotion requests
- Leave Without Pay (LWOP)
- Retirement processing
- Resignation handling
- Change of cadre
- Service extensions
- Termination and dismissal
- Employee complaints
- Reports and analytics
- System administration

### 1.3 Who Uses CSMS?

**Nine User Roles:**

| Role        | Full Name                      | Primary Functions                                  |
| ----------- | ------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **HRO**     | HR Officer                     | Submit HR requests for institution                 |
| **HHRMD**   | Head of HR Management Division | Approve all request types                          |
| **HRMO**    | HR Management Officer          | Approve HR requests (not disciplinary)             |
| **DO**      | Disciplinary Officer           | Handle complaints and disciplinary matters         |
| **EMP**     | Employee                       | Submit complaints, view own profile                |
| **PO**      | Planning Officer               | Generate reports for planning                      |
| **Auditor** | System Auditor                 | Review audit logs and all activities of the system |
| **CSCS**    | CSC Secretary                  | Executive oversight and monitoring                 |
| **HRRP**    | HR Responsible Personnel       | Monitor institutional HR activities                |
| **ADMIN**   | System Administrator           | Manage users and system configuration              |

### 1.4 System Requirements

**To use CSMS, you need:**

- ✅ Computer or laptop (tablet supported)

- ✅ Internet connection (minimum 2 Mbps)

- ✅ Modern web browser:
  - Google Chrome 90+ (Recommended)
  - Mozilla Firefox 88+
  - Microsoft Edge 90+
- ✅ Screen resolution: 1024x768 or higher

- ✅ Valid user account (provided by administrator)

### 1.5 Accessing CSMS

**System URL:** `https://csms.zanajira.go.tz` 

**Supported Languages:**

- English
- Swahili

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 First-Time Login

**Step 1: Navigate to Login Page**

1. Open your web browser
2. Type the CSMS URL in the address bar
3. Press Enter
4. You will see the login page

**Step 2: Enter Your Credentials**

1. Enter your **Username** (provided by administrator)
2. Enter your **Password** (temporary password from welcome email)
3. Click **"Login"** button

**Step 3: Change Temporary Password (First Login Only)**

1. After first login, system will prompt you to change password
2. Enter your temporary password
3. Create a new password following these rules:
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
   - At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
   - At least one number (0-9)
   - At least one special character (!@#$%^&\*)
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click **"Change Password"**

**Example Strong Password:** `Csms@2025!`

### 2.2 Understanding the Dashboard

After successful login, you will see your personalized dashboard:

```
For all users their respective dashboard are attached at the end of this manual.
```

**Dashboard Elements:**

1. **Header:**
   - System logo and name
   - Main navigation menu
   - User profile menu
   - Notification bell 🔔
   
2. **Main Content:**
   - Welcome message
   - Summary statistics (cards/widgets)
   - Recent activities

### 2.3 Navigation Menu

**Main Menu Items:**

### 2.4 Changing Your Password

**Steps:**

1. Click your name in the top right corner
2. Select **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password (must meet requirements)
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click **"Save Changes"**
7. You will receive confirmation message

**Password Tips:**

- ✅ Use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- ✅ Make it at least 8 characters for better security
- ✅ Don't use personal information (name, birthday)
- ✅ Don't share your password with anyone
- ✅ Change password every 60 to 90 days

### 2.5 Forgot Password?

**If you forget your password:**

1. Contact administrator 

2. Administrator will verify your identity

3. Then he will provide you with temporary password.

4. You will need to change this temporary password after your first sign inn. 

5. You will not be able to use the system unless you change your temporary password.

   

### 2.6 Session Timeout

**For security, CSMS automatically logs you out after:**

- **7 minutes of inactivity**

**Warning System:**

- At 6 minutes, you'll see a popup: "Session expiring in 1 minute"
- Options:
  - Click on any menu or move your mouse to stay logged in.
  - Click **"Logout"** to end session
  - Do nothing → Auto-logout at 7 minutes

**If you're logged out:**

- Your work is saved automatically (for most forms)
- Simply log in again to continue

### 2.7 Logging Out

**Always log out when finished:**

1. Click your name (top right)
2. Click **"Logout"**
3. Confirm logout
4. You'll be redirected to login page

**Why logout is important:**

- Protects your account from unauthorized access
- Especially important on shared computers
- Good security practice

#### 3.1.3 HRO: Submitting LWOP Request

**Purpose:** Apply for Leave Without Pay for an employee who needs unpaid leave for an extended period

**Prerequisites:**

- Employee has been confirmed (not on probation)
- No existing pending LWOP request for the same employee
- Required documents available:
  1. Letter of Request (from the institution)
  2. Employee Consent Letter

**Steps:**

1. Click **"LWOP"** from the left navigation menu

2. **Search for Employee:**
   - Enter the ZanID or Payroll Number of the employee
   - Click **"Search"** and the employee details will appear
   - System validates that the employee is not on probation
   - System checks for any existing pending LWOP request
   - If not eligible, system shows an error message

3. **Fill in LWOP Details:**
   - **Start Date:** Enter the date when LWOP begins
   - **End Date:** Enter the date when LWOP ends
   - **Duration:** Automatically calculated by the system (in months)
   - **Reason:** Enter a clear explanation for the leave request

   > ⚠️ **Important:** Maximum LWOP duration is **36 months**. The system will reject requests exceeding this limit.

4. **Upload Required Documents:**
   - Letter of Request (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED
   - Employee Consent Letter (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED

5. **Review Information:**
   - Verify the start and end dates are correct
   - Confirm the duration shown is accurate
   - Check all documents are uploaded successfully

6. **Submit Request:**
   - Click **"Submit Request"**
   - Confirm submission in the dialog
   - You will see a success message with a Request ID
   - Request is routed to HHRMD and HRMO for parallel review

**After Submission:**

- Request appears in your requests list with status: **Pending**
- You can view the request but cannot edit it (unless returned for correction)
- Employee status will automatically update to "On LWOP" upon approval

**Example Scenario:**

```
Employee: Mariam Juma Hamad
Payroll: PRL20210045
Status: Confirmed ✅
Start Date: March 1, 2026
End Date: August 31, 2026
Duration: 6 months ✅ (Within 36-month limit)
Reason: Accompanying spouse on overseas assignment
Action: Submit LWOP request
```

#### 3.1.4 HRO: Submitting Promotion Request

**Purpose:** Request promotion of an employee based on experience or educational advancement

**Prerequisites:**

- Employee must have minimum 2 years of service
- Employee must not be on probation or on LWOP
- No existing pending promotion request for the employee
- Required documents available based on promotion type

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Promotion"** from the left navigation menu

2. **Search for Employee:**
   - Enter the ZanID or Payroll Number of the employee
   - Click **"Search"** and the employee details will appear
   - System validates eligibility (minimum 2 years of service, correct status)
   - If not eligible, an error message is displayed

3. **Select Promotion Type:**

   Choose one of the two promotion types:

   - **Experience-Based Promotion:** For employees promoted based on years of service and performance
   - **Education-Based Promotion (Education Advancement):** For employees who have obtained new qualifications

4. **Enter Proposed Cadre:**
   - Type the new cadre/grade the employee is being promoted to

5. **Upload Required Documents:**

   **If Experience-Based:**
   - Performance Appraisal — Year 1 (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED
   - Performance Appraisal — Year 2 (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED
   - Performance Appraisal — Year 3 (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED
   - CSC Promotion Form (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED
   - Letter of Request (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED

   **If Education-Based:**
   - Certificate / Qualification Document (PDF, max 1MB) — Optional
   - TCU Equivalence Form (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED if employee studied outside the country
   - Letter of Request (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED

   > ℹ️ **Note:** If the employee studied outside the country, tick the **"Studied Outside Country"** checkbox. The TCU form will then become mandatory.

6. **Review Information:**
   - Confirm the promotion type selected is correct
   - Verify the proposed cadre is accurate
   - Check all documents are uploaded successfully

7. **Submit Request:**
   - Click **"Submit Request"**
   - Confirm submission in the dialog
   - Success message with Request ID will appear
   - Request is routed to HHRMD and HRMO for parallel review

**After Submission:**

- Request appears in your requests list with status: **Pending**
- You can view the request but cannot edit it (unless returned for correction)

**Example Scenario:**

```
Employee: Hassan Ali Mohammed
Payroll: PRL20190023
Service Years: 6 years ✅ (Minimum 2 years met)
Status: Confirmed ✅
Promotion Type: Experience-Based
Proposed Cadre: Senior Administrative Officer Grade II
Documents: 3 performance appraisals + CSC form + request letter
Action: Submit promotion request
```

#### 3.1.5 HRO: Submitting Change of Cadre Request

**Purpose:** Request a change in an employee's occupational category/cadre, typically due to new qualifications obtained

**Prerequisites:**

- Employee must have minimum 3 years of service
- Employee must have valid status (not on LWOP, not on probation)
- No existing pending cadre change request for the employee
- Required documents available

> ⚠️ **Note:** Change of Cadre requests are reviewed by **HHRMD only** — HRMO does not review this type of request.

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Change of Cadre"** from the left navigation menu

2. **Search for Employee:**
   - Enter the ZanID or Payroll Number of the employee
   - Click **"Search"** and the employee details will appear
   - System validates minimum 3 years of service
   - System checks for any existing pending cadre change request
   - If not eligible, an error message is displayed

3. **Fill in Cadre Change Details:**
   - **New Cadre and Grade:** Enter the new cadre the employee is moving to
   - **Reason for Cadre Change / Qualifications:** Describe the qualifications or reason for the cadre change
   - **Studied Outside Country:** Tick this checkbox if the employee obtained their qualification from outside Tanzania/Zanzibar

4. **Upload Required Documents:**
   - Letter of Request (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED
   - Certificate / Academic Qualification (PDF, max 1MB) — Optional
   - TCU Equivalence Form (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED if "Studied Outside Country" is ticked

5. **Review Information:**
   - Confirm the new cadre is correctly entered
   - Check the reason/qualifications description is complete
   - Verify all uploaded documents are clear and readable

6. **Submit Request:**
   - Click **"Submit Request"**
   - Confirm submission in the dialog
   - Success message with Request ID will appear
   - Request is sent to HHRMD for review and decision

**After Submission:**

- Request appears in your requests list with status: **Pending**
- You can view the request but cannot edit it (unless returned for correction)
- Only HHRMD can approve or reject this request type

**Example Scenario:**

```
Employee: Zuwena Said Omar
Payroll: PRL20180067
Service Years: 7 years ✅ (Minimum 3 years met)
Current Cadre: Clerical Officer
New Cadre: Administrative Officer
Reason: Obtained Bachelor's degree in Public Administration
Studied Outside Country: No
Documents: Letter of Request + Certificate
Action: Submit cadre change request
```

#### 3.1.6 HRO: Submitting Retirement Request

**Purpose:** Initiate the retirement process for an employee who is eligible to retire

**Prerequisites:**

- Employee must meet the minimum age requirement for the selected retirement type
- Required documents must be available
- No existing pending retirement request for the employee

**Retirement Type Age Requirements:**

| Retirement Type      | Minimum Age | Notes                              |
| -------------------- | ----------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Compulsory           | 60 years    | Mandatory at age 60                |
| Voluntary            | 55 years    | Employee's choice to retire early  |
| On Grounds of Illness| Any age     | Medical certificate required       |

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Retirement"** from the left navigation menu

2. **Search for Employee:**
   - Enter the ZanID or Payroll Number of the employee
   - Click **"Search"** and the employee details will appear
   - System validates age requirements for the selected retirement type
   - System checks for any existing pending retirement request
   - If not eligible, an error message is displayed

3. **Select Retirement Type:**
   - **Compulsory:** Employee is 60 years of age or above
   - **Voluntary:** Employee is 55 years of age or above and chooses to retire
   - **On Grounds of Illness:** Employee has a medical condition requiring retirement

4. **Enter Retirement Details:**
   - **Proposed Retirement Date:** The date the employee will officially retire
   - **Illness Description:** (Required only for "On Grounds of Illness") — Describe the medical condition

5. **Upload Required Documents:**
   - Letter of Request (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED
   - Medical Certificate (PDF, max 1MB) — ✅ REQUIRED for "On Grounds of Illness" only

6. **Review Information:**
   - Confirm the retirement type is correct
   - Verify the proposed retirement date
   - Ensure all required documents are uploaded

7. **Submit Request:**
   - Click **"Submit Request"**
   - Confirm submission in the dialog
   - Success message with Request ID will appear
   - Request is routed to HHRMD and HRMO for review

**After Submission:**

- Request appears in your requests list with status: **Pending**
- You can view the request but cannot edit it (unless returned for correction)
- Employee status will automatically update to "Retired" upon final approval

**Example Scenario:**

```
Employee: Juma Suleiman Mwalimu
Payroll: PRL20000012
Date of Birth: January 15, 1966
Current Age: 60 years ✅ (Compulsory retirement age met)
Retirement Type: Compulsory
Proposed Retirement Date: April 1, 2026
Documents: Letter of Request
Action: Submit retirement request
```

**After Submission:**

```
Employee: Ali Rashid Hassan
Payroll: PRL19950034
```

#### 3.1.10 HRO: Submitting Dismissal Request (Confirmed Employee)

**Purpose:** Request dismissal of a confirmed employee on disciplinary grounds following due process

**Prerequisites:**

- Employee must be a **Confirmed** employee (not on probation)
- Due process must have been followed (warnings issued, employee given opportunity to respond)
- No existing pending dismissal/termination request for the employee
- Required documents must be available

> ⚠️ **Important:** Dismissal applies **only to confirmed employees**. For probationary employees, use the **Termination** request type instead. Due to the serious nature of dismissal, detailed supporting documentation is required.

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Termination & Dismissal"** from the left navigation menu

2. **Search for Employee:**
   - Enter the ZanID or Payroll Number of the employee
   - Click **"Search"** and the employee details will appear
   - System automatically detects employee status
   - If the employee is **Confirmed**, the Dismissal form is displayed
   - If the employee is not confirmed, an error message is shown

3. **Fill in Dismissal Details:**
   - **Reason for Dismissal:** Provide a thorough explanation of the grounds for dismissal (e.g., gross misconduct, criminal activity, unauthorized prolonged absence, breach of trust). Be specific and factual.

4. **Upload Required Documents:**

   | Document | Requirement |
   |---|---|
   | Letter of Request | ✅ REQUIRED |
   | Misconduct Evidence | Optional (photographs, reports, etc.) |
   | Summon Notice | Optional (copy of official summons issued to employee) |
   | Suspension Letter | Optional (if employee was suspended during investigation) |
   | Warning Letter(s) | Optional (all prior warning letters issued) |
   | Employee Explanation Letter | Optional (employee's written response/defense) |
   | Other Additional Documents | Optional (any other relevant documentation) |

   > ℹ️ While many documents are optional, providing as much documentation as possible strengthens the case and speeds up review.

5. **Review Information:**
   - Ensure the dismissal reason is detailed and factual
   - Verify that due process documentation is included
   - Check all uploaded files are clear and readable

6. **Submit Request:**
   - Click **"Submit Request"**
   - Confirm submission in the dialog
   - Success message with Request ID will appear
   - Request is routed to HHRMD for review and decision

**After Submission:**

- "Session expired" message
- Automatically logged out

**Cause:**

- 10 minutes of inactivity

**Solutions:**

1. Simply login again
2. Your work is usually auto-saved
3. To prevent:
   - Click "Stay Logged In" when warning appears
   - Stay active (click, type, navigate)

---

**Issue 3: File Upload Failed**

**Symptoms:**

- "File upload failed" error
- Upload bar stuck at 0%

**Possible Causes:**

1. File too large (>2MB for documents, >1MB for complaints)
2. Wrong file type (not PDF for documents)
3. Network issue
4. Server issue

**Solutions:**

**If File Too Large:**

1. Compress PDF file:
   - Use online tool (e.g., ilovepdf.com)
   - Or Adobe Acrobat
   - Target: Under 2MB
2. Re-upload compressed file

**If Wrong File Type:**

1. Convert document to PDF
2. Use Microsoft Word: Save As → PDF
3. Or use online converter
4. Re-upload PDF

**If Network Issue:**

1. Check internet connection
2. Try again when connection is stable
3. Try different browser

**If Server Issue:**

1. Wait 5 minutes
2. Try again
3. If persists, contact support

---

**Issue 4: Cannot See Employee/Request**

**Symptoms:**

- Employee/request not appearing in list
- Search returns no results

**Possible Causes:**

1. Wrong institution (for HRO/HRRP)
2. Wrong filters applied
3. Employee/request doesn't exist
4. Insufficient permissions

**Solutions:**

**Check Filters:**

1. Click "Clear Filters"
2. Try search again

**Verify Institution (HRO/HRRP):**

1. Check which institution you're assigned to
2. You can ONLY see your institution's data
3. If employee is in different institution, contact their HRO

**Verify Permissions:**

1. Check your role
2. Some data restricted by role
3. For example: HRMO cannot see complaints

**Verify Existence:**

1. Check spelling of name/payroll
2. Try partial search
3. Ask colleague to verify

---

**Issue 5: Report Generation Failed**

**Symptoms:**

- "Report generation failed" error
- Very long wait time (>5 minutes)

**Possible Causes:**

1. Too much data requested
2. Server busy
3. Invalid parameters
4. Network issue

**Solutions:**

**Reduce Data Range:**

1. Narrow date range
2. Filter by institution
3. Smaller data = faster report

**Check Parameters:**

1. Verify date range valid (From < To)
2. Check all required fields filled
3. Try different format (PDF vs Excel)

**Try Again:**

1. Wait a few minutes
2. Try during off-peak hours (early morning)
3. Contact support if persists

---

**Issue 6: Forgot Username**

**Symptoms:**

- Don't remember username

**Solution:**

1. Check welcome email (when account created)
2. Contact administrator
3. Provide:
   - Your full name
   - Email address
   - Institution
4. Administrator will provide username

---

**Issue 7: Dashboard Widgets Not Loading**

**Symptoms:**

- Blank widgets
- Loading spinner doesn't stop
- "Error loading data"

**Possible Causes:**

1. Network issue
2. Server issue
3. Browser cache issue

**Solutions:**

**Refresh Page:**

1. Press F5 or click browser refresh
2. Wait for page to reload

**Clear Browser Cache:**

1. Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Click "Clear data"
4. Reload CSMS

**Try Different Browser:**

1. If Chrome fails, try Firefox or Edge
2. If works in different browser, clear original browser cache

**Check Network:**

1. Verify internet connection
2. Try accessing other websites
3. Contact IT support if general network issue

- ✅ Cookies: Enabled
- ✅ Pop-ups: Allow for CSMS site
- ✅ Downloads: Allowed

**To Check JavaScript:**

1. Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Site Settings → JavaScript → Allowed
2. Firefox: about:config → javascript.enabled → true

**To Enable Cookies:**

1. Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies → Allow all cookies (or allow for CSMS site)
2. Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies → Accept cookies

### 5.4 Tips for Better Performance

**For Faster Page Loading:**

1. Use Chrome (fastest)
2. Close unnecessary browser tabs
3. Clear browser cache weekly
4. Disable unnecessary browser extensions
5. Use wired internet (not WiFi) if possible

### Appendix C: Status Definitions

**Employee Statuses:**

- **On Probation:** Employee in initial 12-18 month evaluation period
- **Confirmed:** Employee who passed probation
- **On LWOP:** Employee on approved leave without pay
- **Retired:** Employee who has retired
- **Resigned:** Employee who resigned voluntarily
- **Terminated:** Confirmed employee separated due to disciplinary action
- **Dismissed:** Probationary employee separated
- **Deceased:** Employee passed away

**Request Statuses:**

- **Pending:** Awaiting approval decision
- **Approved:** Request approved by approver
- **Rejected:** Request rejected with reason
- **Returned:** Sent back to HRO for corrections

**Complaint Statuses:**

- **Pending:** Awaiting DO assignment
- **Under Review:** Being investigated by DO
- **Resolved:** Complaint investigated and closed
- **Rejected:** Complaint rejected
